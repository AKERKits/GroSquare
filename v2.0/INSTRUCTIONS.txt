"INSTRUCTIONS.txt"

Instructions for exporting to toolpath generation tools from SolidWorks parametric CAD drawings.

GREGOIRE DURRENS
14/04/2016


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


1 - Open all *.SLDPRT files in SolidWorks

2 - Open "kit_name".SLDASM in SolidWorks

3 - Update your parameters in the equations_"kitName".txt file. Save the text file.

4 - Rebuild the "kit_name".SLDASM in SolidWorks. Needs two rebuilt iterations so click twice.

5 - Open the "kit_name".SLDDRW file in SolidWorks. Select the "For toolpath Generation" Sheet (bottom left of screen).

6 - Export your 2D files for toolpath generation in whichever software you want.
